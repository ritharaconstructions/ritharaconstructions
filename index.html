Welcome to Rithara constructions

10 Years Experience

We specialize in delivering high-quality construction services that cover every stage of a building project — from the foundation to the final finish. Our expertise spans a wide range of disciplines, including masonry, plumbing, electrical, centering, tiling, woodwork and cupboards, fabrication, welding, and painting. Whether you're building a new structure or renovating an existing one, our team is committed to craftsmanship, reliability, and on-time project delivery.

We provide end-to-end construction services with unmatched professionalism and quality.With a team of skilled craftsmen and experienced technicians, we bring structure, style, and functionality to every space we work on. we’re your one-stop solution for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our Services

We Provide Services

Strong structures begin with solid masonry. Our skilled masons lay the groundwork for homes, buildings, and commercial spaces using time-tested techniques and quality materials. From brickwork to concrete block walls, we ensure every wall is straight, secure, and built to last.

Masonry Works

+

We provide sturdy and accurate formwork for concrete structures such as columns, slabs, beams, and staircases. Our centering work ensures perfect alignment, stability, and support during construction.

Centering (Formwork)

Our licensed electricians handle wiring, circuit installations, lighting, and more — ensuring safe, efficient, and code-compliant electrical systems for homes, offices, and industrial sites.

Electrical Services

+

We offer complete plumbing solutions, including water supply installation, drainage systems, sanitary fittings, and repair services. Our team ensures efficient and hygienic plumbing that meets all safety and durability standards.

Plumbing Services

Wood & Cupboard Works

Fabrication

Welding Services
